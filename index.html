About

I shoot with intent,
edit with soul

I'm Alex Voss — a visual storyteller based between New York and wherever the light is best. With a camera in hand and wanderlust in my veins, I've spent the last eight years chasing extraordinary moments in ordinary places.

My work lives at the intersection of fine-art photography and content creation — built for screens, but crafted with the patience of a darkroom printer. I work with brands, musicians, travel companies, and humans who believe their story deserves to be told beautifully.
